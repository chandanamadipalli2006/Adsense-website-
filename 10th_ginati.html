Today, we’re diving into the world of Hindi numbers. Learning numbers in any language can seem daunting, but we’ve got some easy tricks to help you remember them effortlessly.

Hindi Numbers 1 to 10

  • 1 - Ek
  • 2 - Do
  • 3 - Teen
  • 4 - Chaar
  • 5 - Paanch
  • 6 - Chhah
  • 7 - Saat
  • 8 - Aath
  • 9 - Nau
  • 10 - Das

Hindi Numbers 11 to 15

  • 11 - Gyarah
  • 12 - Barah
  • 13 - Terah
  • 14 - Chaudah
  • 15 - Pandrah

Notice how 'Barah' (12) and 'Terah' (13) have the 'rah' ending. Once you catch the pattern, it gets easier.
